CMC Quad Ranking

  1. Mid Quad

    I live in this quad, which has a central location adjacent to Parents Field. There are a mix of singles, doubles, and even triples. There is working air conditioning as well, which is a huge benefit

  2. North Quad

    This is the most social quad, located on the boulevard that connects the cube to Bauer Center. There is great social life here, but no air conditioning

  3. South Quad

    This is the quietest quad, with many singles. Athletes and introverts live here for the most part
